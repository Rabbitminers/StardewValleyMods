# Where's My Stuff?

A little mod to help you search your ~~hoarding problem~~ nicely organized chests.

This mod is heavily inspired by [Find Me](https://modrinth.com/mod/findme) a Minecraft mod with similar functionality. Turns out I can't find anything in either.

## Features

- Press 'P' (configurable) to search nearby chests for the item under your cursor or in your hand.
- Press 'O' (configurable) to open a menu to type the name of an item to find.
- Fuzzy search helps even if you can't spell (we’ve all been there)
- Highlights matching chests and shows their contents
